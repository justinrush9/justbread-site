Account

Manage your subscription

Enter the email address you used when you subscribed. We'll open the secure customer portal where you can manage everything yourself.

From the portal you can:

  • Pause or cancel your subscription
  • Update your payment method or billing address
  • View upcoming and past invoices
  • Update your contact information
Need to change your loaf quantity or delivery frequency?
Cancel your current subscription in the portal, then place a new order at justbread.shop/order with your updated preferences. Your first new delivery will reflect the changes.
